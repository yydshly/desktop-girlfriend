# Prompt: 房间背景

用途：生成统一桌面场景背景，作为走路、眼神、待机等卡片底图。

## 中文提示词

```text
夜晚的温馨电脑桌房间，双显示器，木质书桌，暖色台灯，小盆栽，书架和窗帘，柔和景深，干净整洁，适合作为桌面 AI 伙伴展示背景，画面中心留出角色站立空间，电影感灯光，暖色与深蓝色平衡，高质量背景图，不要人物，不要文字，不要 logo。
```

## English Prompt

```text
A cozy night-time computer desk room, dual monitors, wooden desk, warm desk lamp, small plant, bookshelf and curtains, soft depth of field, clean and tidy. Designed as a background for a desktop AI companion showcase, with empty space in the center for a character. Cinematic warm lighting balanced with deep blue shadows, high-quality background image, no people, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
people, character, text, logo, watermark, messy room, horror lighting, overexposed lamp, distorted monitors, low quality
```

## 输出建议

- 横图，16:9。
- 先用 1920x1080。
- 背景最好略虚化，角色会更突出。
